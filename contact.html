Description

Since 2018, design projects include product and service design, UI design. In the field of art, I have a particular focus on creating installations that are visually and emotionally relevant to the human sensory experience. All projects have a clear intention and purpose; they are feminist, radical and elegant, practical and educational.

During 2018-2020, I organized and participated in live music performances in a style close to post-rock and neo-classical.And I will take this musical obsession into the future with artistic research, sound installations, etc.
