Feminist product design has a large market gap.

The unique physiological needs of women should not be ignored by design, but should be studied in a targeted manner.






Five essential oils based on aromatherapy have been formulated to target the different symptoms of Premenstrual syndrome.
Product system can be registered as a user and the user can be recommended the applicable essential oil by selecting their symptoms.
Each essential oil has its own use, purchase page, with accompanying immersive music.
Users exchange experiences, view scientific articles and create women-friendly communities on the support platform.
