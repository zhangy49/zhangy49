Inspired by a lung disease caused by depression.



In ancient Chinese medicine, the organs are inextricably linked to emotions; and in both eastern and western systems of human language, we often express our emotions in the moment by describing how our organs feel.



In my installation, the biological changes in the organs affected by emotions are translated into the colour, number, direction of movement, speed and frequency of flicker of light.



These lights are projected on the wall through the textured transparent surface to create a visualisation of the physiological agency.
